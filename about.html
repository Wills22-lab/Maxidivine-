Our Story

Founded in November 2017 and headquartered along the Ejisu Kwamo Highway in Kumasi, Maxidivine Limited Company started its journey as an enterprise focused on cement distribution. In September 2018, the company expanded into retailing, laying the foundation for rapid and sustainable growth. In 2023, Maxidivine transitioned into a limited liability company, further solidifying its corporate structure and commitment to excellence. Today, Maxidivine operates five outlets for cement and building materials distribution, a block factory producing about 3000 quality concrete blocks per day, a construction firm (MD Construction and Estate Limited) and the largest sanitary ware and tiles showroom¬†in¬†Kumasi.
